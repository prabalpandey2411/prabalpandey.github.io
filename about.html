Electronics & Telecom Engineer · Author · Web Consultant

I'm Prabal Pandey — born and raised in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, where my love for both language and logic took root. I pursued my engineering studies in Pune and now live in Navi Mumbai, where I work with Axis Bank, bringing analytical thinking to the financial sector.

But numbers and circuits are only half the story. Writing has always been the other half. Over the years I've published four books on Amazon — from poetry and philosophy to personal finance — and contributed research papers to peer-reviewed journals on topics from 5G to sustainable energy.

I believe the best ideas live at the intersection of technical precision and human expression. Whether I'm writing about self-discovery, explaining a networking protocol, or building a website for a local business, I'm trying to do the same thing: make something complex feel clear and worth engaging with.

When I'm not working or writing, you might find me exploring a new city, chasing the perfect cup of chai, or crafting a shayari that says in two lines what paragraphs can't.
