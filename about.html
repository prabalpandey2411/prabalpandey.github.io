Electronics & Telecom Engineer · Author · Aspiring Data Analyst

I'm Prabal Pandey — born and raised in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, where my love for both language and logic took root. I pursued my engineering studies in Pune and now live in Navi Mumbai. I bring a structured, analytical mindset to everything I take on, and I'm currently looking for my next role in Data Analytics and PL/SQL development — places where that mindset turns into something measurable.

But numbers and circuits are only half the story. Writing has always been the other half. Over the years I've published four books on Amazon — from poetry and philosophy to personal finance — and contributed research papers to peer-reviewed journals on topics from 5G to sustainable energy.

I believe the best ideas live at the intersection of technical precision and human expression. Whether I'm writing about self-discovery, explaining a networking protocol, or querying a dataset for the story hidden in it, I'm trying to do the same thing: make something complex feel clear and worth engaging with.

When I'm not working or writing, you might find me exploring a new city, chasing the perfect cup of chai, or crafting a shayari that says in two lines what paragraphs can't.

"Where there's a will, there's a way" — and usually a workaround, a second cup of chai, and a rewritten query that finally runs.
